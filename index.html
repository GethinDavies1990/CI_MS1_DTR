How
Duck's Tap Room
Was Born

2010 - Home Brew

Started home brewing and delivering to friends and family for feedback.

2012 - Warehouse was Purchased

We outgrew our home brewery and required bigger space to meet the demand of our customers

2016 - Our First Tap Room

After many years of steady growth and supplying local pubs and restaurants, We made the jump and made our own tap room
